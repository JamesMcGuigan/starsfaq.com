    • Econ Settings in Race Design
      • 1000   12/9/17-g   11/3/16   means:
          1 resource per 1000 colonists

          10 factories produce 12 resources per year
          Factories require 9 resources to build
          10k colonists can operate 17 factories
          Factories cost 1kt less germanium to build

          10 mines produce up to 11kt minerals per year
          Mines require 3 resources to build
          10k colonists can operate 16 mines
